  • Leon, Harmon. "Mind-Reading Tech Is Dangerously Close to Becoming a Reality." Observer, 9 Sept. 2019, observer.com/2019/09/facebook-elon-musk-neuralink-mind-reading-tech-future/. Accessed 11 Sept. 2019. This article is about how Facebook is researching how to read people's minds, and how Neuralink is scary because it might allow companies to read our minds rather than just our social media.
  • Lopatto, Elizabeth. "Elon Musk unveils Neuralink's plans for brain-reading 'threads' and a robot to insert them." A good summary of information that was available after Neuralinks announcement. Includes excerpts from different people regarding the news.
  • Musk, Elon. "An integrated brain-machine interface platform with thousands of channels." BioRxiv the preprint server for biology, pp. 0-12. BioRxiv, doi:10.1101/703801. Accessed 11 Sept. 2019. An official unpublished paper by neural link detailing the science that goes into neural link, and how it works. This paper has yet to be peer reviewed.
  • "Neuralink | Complete Presentation by Elon Musk 2019." Youtube, uploaded by ADVEXON TV, 20 July 2019, www.youtube.com/watch?v=dXbShyPQrhM&t=1s. Accessed 11 Sept. 2019. The complete video of Elon Musk's presentation / reveal of Neuralink.
  • "Neuralink - Merging Brain and Machine." Youtube, uploaded by Cold Fusion, 19 July 2019, www.youtube.com/watch?v=9uRK8Delzvk. Accessed 11 Sept. 2019. A good video explaining relevant information regarding Neuralink.
  • Pollack, Andrew. "Paralyzed Man Uses Thoughts to Move a Cursor." New York Times, 13 July 2006, www.nytimes.com/2006/07/13/science/13brain.html. Accessed 11 Sept. 2019. Includes information about a man who was able to move a mouse cursor with his mind among other things with technology that predates Neuralink.
  • Samuel, Sigal. "Brain-reading tech is coming. The law is not ready to protect us." VOX, 30 Aug. 2019, www.vox.com/2019/8/30/20835137/facebook-zuckerberg-elon-musk-brain-mind-reading-neuroethics. Accessed 11 Sept. 2019. Talks about the new tech that can translate a person's thoughts into words, that Neuralink and facebook have teamed up to create. Goes into detail about possible laws that the person who was interviewed thinks should exist.
  • Urban, Tim. "Neuralink and the Brain's Magical Future." Wait but Why, 20 Apr. 2017, waitbutwhy.com/2017/04/neuralink.html. Accessed 12 Sept. 2019. A very long and in depth look at what Neuralink is. A good read if you have time, but very long. Great insider information through interviews, and easy to follow explanations.
